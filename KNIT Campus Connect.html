About KNIT

Excellence in technical education since 1976

Kamla Nehru Institute of Technology (KNIT) is a premier institute established in 1976 under the aegis of the Department of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh. Located in Sultanpur, KNIT has consistently maintained high standards in technical education and research.

Over the years, the institute has evolved into a center of excellence, producing skilled engineers and technologists who contribute significantly to various sectors of the economy.
